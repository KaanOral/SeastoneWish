About Us

A bit about your Sea Stone Hosts "We" are Kaan, Flow, and Logan. We are part time residents of the Long Beach Peninsula. For the past years, we have been cultivating our relationship with these lands and waters, and honing our coastal skills. In 2024 we began inviting our extended kinfolk and community to come and gather with us at our coastal home. In August of 2024 we hosted a small and mighty group of folks for the Sea Stone Seeders Camp. During that camp, we collectively dreamt up the vision for the Sea Stone Family Gathering. It's been an honor to invite our friends, family, and extended community to join us here to gather, learn, and nourish our relationships with sea.

See you at the Sea!

Enroll

We've made the decision to
TRANSITION OUR GATHERINGS AND CAMPOUTS TO A PRIVATE MEMBERSHIP STRUCTURE.

We've created the Coast & Crest Cahoots club, a private membership group, as the container through which we will offer camps such as The Sea Stone Family Gathering and Clam Camp, as well as other various camps and group activities we may develop along the way.

Clam Camp

We’ll be harvesting the Pacific Razor Clam, learning all about clam anatomy + life cycle+ clam cleaning + instruction in clam culinary dishes + clam pressure canning.

Chinook

Before moving too far ahead in our cultivation as a community which gathers and harvests on Chinook lands, its important to us to amplify the voices of the Chinook Nation and to do what we can to support them in their ongoing efforts to regain their right to federal recognition
